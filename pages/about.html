Our Story

Founded in 2010, TechAuto began with a simple vision: to create vehicles that seamlessly integrate cutting-edge technology with exceptional driving experiences. What started as a small team of engineers and designers in a Silicon Valley garage has grown into a global mobility company revolutionizing the automotive industry.

Our journey has been marked by relentless innovation and a commitment to excellence. From our first electric prototype to our autonomous driving systems, we've consistently pushed the boundaries of what's possible in automotive design and engineering.

Today, with facilities across three continents and a diverse team of over 5,000 passionate individuals, we continue to pursue our mission of creating sustainable, intelligent, and exhilarating mobility solutions for the world.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive everything we do

Mission

To accelerate the world's transition to sustainable mobility through innovative technology, exceptional design, and unforgettable driving experiences that enrich people's lives and protect our planet.

Vision

To create a future where transportation is intelligent, efficient, and accessible to all - where vehicles seamlessly integrate with our lives, enhance our communities, and leave minimal environmental impact.

Promise

To deliver vehicles that exceed expectations, experiences that delight, and innovations that push boundaries - all while maintaining the highest standards of integrity, sustainability, and customer care.
